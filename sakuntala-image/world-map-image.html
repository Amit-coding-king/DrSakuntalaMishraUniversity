    Large World Map


    The image of World Map above is centered on Europe and Africa. Moreover, this World Map image focuses on the location of various countries around the world. Further, this map points out the names and geographical boundaries of these countries. Representation of a spherical earth on a flat surface requires a map projection. This transformation of spherical earth on to a flat plane produces some distortion, which cannot be avoided. In the above World Map image, the Mercator projection has been used. Though it creates distortion, it is minimal distortion at the equator.
